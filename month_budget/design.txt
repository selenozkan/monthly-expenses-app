Title: Monthly Expense Report
Description: The goal of this project is for a user to enter their income for a given month,
and the expenses (based on specific categories)
they made during that month. The program will then give an output of total expense, category % breakdown and how much money is left
with simple flags/insights.
Objects: MonthlyBudget:
            income: float
            period: string
            expenses: dict
            add_expense()
            total_expenses()
            remaining()
            percent_of_expense_by_category()
            savings_rate() = remaining / income

Categories:
- Housing (Rent, Utilities)
- Food (Groceries, eating out)
- Transport (public transport, taxi, fuel)
- Entertainment (shopping, socializing, subscriptions)
- Health (doctor, pharmacy, insurance, fitness)
- Extras
